Timeless designs made with recycled silver


Dodola Design is a passion project, creating pieces to wear and cherish.
We pride ourselves on each piece being one of a kind. Each piece is hand-made by our silversmith, using stones selected for their quality and unique charms.
Because this is a passion project, we sadly do not have as much time or money as we would like to dedicate to the craft and very much appreciate the support we've received to keep this project going!
